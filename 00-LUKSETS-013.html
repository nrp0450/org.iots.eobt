Set 13: John Prepares the Way
(Chapter 3)
13
21-22 So when the crowds were coming to John to receive the washing ceremony, at one point Jesus also came. And while He was talking to God, the skies opened up and the Divine Spirit appeared in physical form and came down onto Jesus the way a dove would come down. And this voice boomed from the sky and said, "You are my Son, I love you very much and I am very pleased with you."
23-38 Now Jesus was about 30 years old when he began His ministry. It was believed by everyone that he was the son of Joseph, who was the son of Heli, who was the son of Matthat, who was the son of Levi, who was the son of Melki, who was the son of Jannai, who was the son of Joseph, who was the son of Mattathias, who was the son of Amos, who was the son of Nahum, who was the son of Esli, who was the son of Naggai, who was the son of Maath, who was the son of Mattathias, who was the son of Semein, who was the son of Josech, who was the son of Joda, who was the son of Joanan, who was the on of Rhesa, who was the son of Zerubbabel, who was the son of Shealtiel. who was the son of Neri, who was the son of Melki, who was the son of Addi, who was the son of Cosam, who was the son of Elmadam, who was the son of Er.
